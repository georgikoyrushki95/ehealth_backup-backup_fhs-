Terms of use

You can use this app for:

  • Searching for medical conditions, symptoms, treatments, through three different online resources - Bing, Medline Plus, HealthGov.
  • Save different resources into categories, which can be either shared or private with other users.
  • Maintain a profile containing a username, email and a profile picture.
  • Browse through other people's shared categories and pages.

Disclaimer

This app was developed for a university project by students from the University of Glasgow. Users of this app are warned that there are inevitable risks in terms of bugs and faulty behaviour.
